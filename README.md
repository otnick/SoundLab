## Introduction

SoundLab is a musical XR experience designed to immerse users in an ambient environment where they can experiment with looped sounds and explore how different audio elements harmonize. The platform encourages creativity and interaction, allowing users to layer, modify, and blend sounds in real time.

The problem identified was the lack of accessible and intuitive tools for musical experimentation in XR environments, making it difficult for users to explore soundscapes without technical barriers.

The proposed solution provides an engaging and user-friendly interface that empowers users to create and manipulate music loops, fostering creativity and making musical exploration more approachable in XR.

## Design Process

We began by brainstorming ideas and pitching them to each other, refining concepts collaboratively. For interactions and core concepts, we used the "Crazy 8" technique to rapidly sketch and explore multiple possibilities. This helped us identify promising directions and prioritize features for development.

- **Brainstorming:** Collaborative sessions to generate and share project ideas.
- **Crazy 8:** Rapid sketching exercise to explore interaction concepts.
- **Concept Selection:** Evaluated and discussed sketches to choose the most effective solutions.
